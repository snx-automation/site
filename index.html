Why Silicon Nexus

A place engineers actually wanted to exist

Most engineering work happens alone - a circuit in Cadence, a repo nobody else sees, a question with no one around to answer it. Silicon Nexus | Circuits & Code exists to change that.

It's a Discord server and GitHub organization where engineers collaborate on real projects, share ideas and resources, review each other's work, and study side by side - whether that's writing code, designing circuits, or just staying consistent.

Nexus Study Lab, the focus timer you've seen throughout this page, is one small piece of that - built by the community, for the community.
